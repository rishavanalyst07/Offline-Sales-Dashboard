# Offline-Sales-Dashboard

Description - Developed an offline Power BI Sales Dashboard for Leeford Healthcare that unified Primary & Secondary Sales KPIs, improving sales visibility, forecasting accuracy, distributor performance tracking, and inventory planning.

*1. Project Overview / Problem Statement*

“This project was a Sales Performance Dashboard developed for sales and management teams to monitor primary sales, secondary sales, target achievement, distributor performance, and stock availability across different regions and states. Before the dashboard, sales data was analyzed manually through multiple reports, making it difficult for management to track performance, identify underperforming regions, and take timely actions. The objective was to provide a centralized view of sales performance and business KPIs for faster decision-making.”

*2. Your Role and Contribution*

I worked as a Data Analyst and was responsible for data extraction, data validation, dashboard development, KPI creation, and report automation.
My contribution included creating Power BI dashboards, developing DAX measures for sales KPIs, validating data against source systems, building interactive visualizations, and supporting stakeholders with ad-hoc reporting and analysis. I also worked closely with business users to understand reporting requirements and implemented dashboard enhancements based on their feedback.”

*3. Data Sources and Tools*

“The sales data was stored in SQL Server databases. I used SQL queries to extract and validate the required datasets. Power Query was used for data transformation and Power BI was used for dashboard development. DAX was used for KPI calculations and business metrics.”

*4. Process / Methodology*

“The process started with collecting and validating sales and distributor data from source systems. I used SQL and Power Query to clean and transform the data before creating a structured data model in Power BI.
After data preparation, I developed DAX measures for sales KPIs, target achievement percentages, growth calculations, and trend analysis. Interactive visualizations and filters were then created, followed by user testing, validation, and deployment.”

*5. Key Features of the Solution*

•Analyzed three key KPIs: Media Air Date → Client Bill Creation, Vendor Invoice Date → Invoice Receipt, and Invoice Receipt →
Invoice Booking to measure end-to-end billing efficiency.
•Designed delay bucket analysis (0–30, 31–60, 61–90, 90+ days) to identify billing bottlenecks, overdue invoices, and process gaps.
•Built vendor-level performance visuals to highlight high-delay vendors impacting overall billing cycle time.
•Delivered transaction-level visibility for Finance and Media teams to support invoice follow-ups, cash-flow monitoring, and
payment prioritization.

*6. Impact and Results*

“The dashboard provided management with a single source of truth for sales monitoring and performance tracking.
It reduced manual reporting efforts, improved visibility into sales trends, helped identify underperforming distributors and regions, and enabled faster business decisions. Management could quickly monitor target achievement, track growth trends, and focus on areas requiring corrective action.”

*7. Key Learnings*

“Through this project, I strengthened my skills in Power BI dashboard development, DAX calculations, sales analytics, and KPI reporting.
I also gained a better understanding of sales operations, distributor management, target tracking, and how analytics supports business decision-making.”

*8. Future Enhancements / Monitoring Support*

“After deployment, I supported dashboard monitoring, data validation, and enhancement requests from stakeholders.
Future enhancements could include:

Sales forecasting using historical trends
Automated alert notifications for low-performing regions
Territory-level performance analysis
Predictive analytics for target achievement
Integration with Power Automate for automated report distribution”
